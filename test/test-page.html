Phone number detection test page

Load this page with the GlobalClick2Dial extension enabled. Detection is limited to US numbers and NANP toll-free (+1) only. Numbers in green-bordered blocks should become clickable links; red-bordered blocks should not.

Should be detected (valid formats)

US with parentheses: Call us at (201) 555-0123 for support.

US with dashes: 201-555-0123

US with dots: 201.555.0123

US with spaces: 201 555 0123

US plain 10 digits: 2015550123

With +1: +1 202 555 0199

Toll-free: 800-234-5678 or 888-555-1212

At start of sentence: 415-555-1212 is the main line.

At end of sentence: The number is 415-555-1212.

After comma: Contact sales, 650-253-0000, for pricing.

In parentheses: Main office (650) 253-0000 is open 9–5.

Eleven-digit US (leading 1): 1-201-555-0123

Should NOT be detected (bad examples)

ID with hyphen (no space): MAT-25082189552

Letters attached: abcd2015550123efgh

Underscore prefix: REF_2015550123 or Order_ID_2015550123

Non-US / non–toll-free +1: 416-555-0123 (Canada) or +44 20 7123 4567 (UK)

Extension suffix (letters after): 201-555-0123ext or 201-555-0123 x100

Part of word: phone2015550123number

Invoice/code style: INV-201-5550123

Long digit string (ID/serial): 123456789012345

Short digit string (too few): 201-55 (only 6 digits)

Date (MM.DD.YYYY, not a phone): 02.24.2026

Edge cases

Hash before (often still valid): Order #201-555-0123

Colon before: Tel: 201-555-0123

Two valid numbers in one line: Call 415-555-1212 or 650-253-0000 for help.

If a number in a green block does not become a link, or a number in a red block does become a link, the detection rules may need adjustment.
