# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 14, 2026**

## Overview

GlobalClick2Dial is a Chrome browser extension that converts phone numbers found on web pages into clickable dial links.

## Data Collection

**We collect no data.** GlobalClick2Dial does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, browsing data, page content, or any other information about you or your activity.

## External Services

**We use no external services or remote code.** All processing is performed entirely within your browser, locally on your device. The extension does not make any network requests, contact any remote servers, or load any scripts from external sources.

## Permissions

The extension requests only the minimum permissions required to function:

- **`storage`** — used solely to save your local settings (dial protocol, custom prefix, and per-site disable list) in Chrome's sync storage.
- **`activeTab`** — used solely to read the current tab's URL in the popup so that the "Disable on this site" button can identify the hostname.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be committed to this repository with a new "Last updated" date.
